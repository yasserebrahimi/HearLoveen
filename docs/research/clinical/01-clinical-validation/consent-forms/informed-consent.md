## WHY IS THIS STUDY BEING DONE?

We are conducting this study to find out if using the HearLoveen mobile application (along with regular speech therapy) helps children with hearing impairment improve their speech skills better than speech therapy alone.

**What is HearLoveen?**
HearLoveen is a mobile app that:
- Records your child's speech practice at home
- Uses artificial intelligence to give immediate feedback
- Makes practice fun with games and rewards
- Shows you reports about your child's progress

## WHAT WILL HAPPEN IF WE PARTICIPATE?

### Study Duration
The study will last **6 months** with a 3-month follow-up (total 9 months).

### Study Procedures

**All participants will:**
1. Continue regular speech therapy (2 sessions per week)
2. Visit the clinic 7 times for assessments
3. Complete questionnaires about satisfaction and motivation

**If assigned to the HearLoveen group, your child will also:**
- Use the HearLoveen app at home for at least 15 minutes per day
- The app will record practice sessions (audio only)
- You will receive progress reports via the app

**If assigned to the control group:**
- Continue only regular speech therapy
- Complete paper-based home practice (as usual)
- Keep a practice diary

### Randomization (Assignment to Groups)
Your child will be randomly assigned (like flipping a coin) to either:
- **Group 1:** Speech therapy only (Control)
- **Group 2:** Speech therapy + HearLoveen app (Treatment)

You have a **50% chance** of being in each group.

### Assessments

| Visit | When | What Happens | Duration |
|-------|------|--------------|----------|
| Screening | Week -2 | Eligibility check, consent | 30 min |
| Baseline | Week 0 | Speech tests, questionnaires | 60 min |
| Visit 1 | Week 6 | Speech tests | 45 min |
| Visit 2 | Week 12 | Speech tests, questionnaires | 60 min |
| Visit 3 | Week 18 | Speech tests | 45 min |
| Visit 4 | Week 24 | Speech tests, questionnaires | 60 min |
| Follow-up | Week 36 | Speech tests | 45 min |

**Total time commitment:** Approximately 7 hours over 9 months

## WHAT ARE THE RISKS?

### Minimal Risks
This study involves minimal risk. Possible risks include:

1. **Fatigue:** Your child may get tired during assessments
   - *Mitigation:* We will take breaks as needed

2. **Frustration:** Your child may feel frustrated if practice is difficult
   - *Mitigation:* Exercises are adapted to your child's level

3. **Privacy:** Audio recordings will be stored
   - *Mitigation:* All data will be encrypted and anonymized

4. **Screen Time:** Additional screen time from using the app
   - *Mitigation:* Recommended usage is only 15 minutes/day

### Data Security
- All data will be stored on secure, encrypted servers
- Your child will be assigned a code number (not their name)
- Only authorized research staff will have access to identifiable information
- Audio recordings will be deleted after analysis (within 2 years)
